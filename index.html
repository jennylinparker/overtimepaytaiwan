FAQ

Is this official?
No. This is an estimate tool only.
Does this apply to every job?
Not always. Rest day work, shift changes, and Article 84-1 jobs may use a different method.
Why is there no rest day option?
To keep the first version simple, this MVP assumes all extra hours are regular workday overtime and splits them into two overtime buckets.
Can I ask a real person?
Yes. Use the Telegram button or leave a question below.
Should I upload my salary slip or ARC?
No. Do not send passport, ARC, bank account, or salary slip photos in this MVP version.
